# The Golden Thread
## A Poetic Cycle by Mark Oriel

![Journey of the Soul](images/Journey%20of%20the%20Soul.png)

*A pilgrim's journey — from the first word spoken in love, through exile and seeking, to the homecoming table where the golden thread ends.*

---

### The Poems

**[Since the Beginning](poems/Since%20the%20Beginning.md)**
*"I counted down every second until I could tell you how loved you are."*

**[Imago Dei](poems/Imago%20Dei.md)**
*"Naked in the beginning, without shame — innocent and transparent, life before blame."*

**[The Exile](poems/The%20Exile.md)**
*"Lovingly nudged, I followed the golden thread, to my heart-home at the water's mouth."*

**[A Name Untold](poems/A%20Name%20Untold.md)**
*"A rooted wanderer, eyes on heaven's brim — I am given my name, the Oaken Pilgrim."*

**[Even Ground](poems/Even%20Ground.md)**
*"The place where I thrive is even ground — an age ago, I never knew the peace I've found."*

**[For Love, In Love, He Made You](poems/For%20Love%2C%20In%20Love%2C%20He%20Made%20You.md)**
*"Since before the beginning of time, He's had you in His holy mind."*

**[Sweet Sophia, Holy Hokhmah](poems/Sweet%20Sophia%2C%20Holy%20Hokhmah.md)**
*"Arms outstretched on a cross, in my place — bringing all nations into Your embrace."*

**[My Peace I Give to You](poems/My%20Peace%20I%20Give%20to%20You.md)**
*"Deepest Shalom, beyond earthly knowing."*

**[Radiant Knight](poems/Radiant%20Knight.md)**
*"The sword of your Word in hand, your righteousness encasing my breast."*

**[Pour Out My Soul](poems/Pour%20Out%20My%20Soul.md)**
*"Loving you is its own reward — it sweetens all I do."*

**[All of My Creatures](poems/All%20of%20My%20Creatures.md)**
*"All of my creatures have my breath — for my children I conquered death."*

**[The Rose Bower](poems/The%20Rose%20Bower.md)**
*"The sweet scent calls me home."*

**[Communion](poems/Communion.md)**
*"An exile no more — communion calls me home, by a golden thread."*
