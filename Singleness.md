Thank you Lord Our God,
for speaking through Becky,
by a preach with appeal broad,
how singles are more than lucky.

Thank you for our calling,
to pursue you with all we are,
to love without falling
brothers and sisters near and far.

Thank you that we are made good,
in body and soul redeemed,
for helping us live as we should
singleness better than it seemed.

Thank you for our married friends,
who love us as their own,
and bringing all to good ends,
no child of yours alone.

Thank you for Your garden,
with flowers of every hue,
and let our hearts not harden,
as we serve You in all we do.
